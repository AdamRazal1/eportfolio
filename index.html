High Performance Data Processing

Scrape Master — Carousell HPC Pipeline

An end-to-end big-data pipeline that crawls 200,000+ marketplace listings from Carousell.com.my, cleans them into a structured dataset, then benchmarks five operations across Pandas, Polars, Multiprocessing, and PySpark. Polars came out fastest — up to ~49× quicker than Pandas. I led the team as Group Leader & Data Engineer.
